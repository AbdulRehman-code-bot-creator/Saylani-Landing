MAKING LIVES BETTER

The Saylani Welfare International Trust, founded in 1999, has made remarkable strides in philanthropy. It operates across various sectors, addressing critical issues like hunger, education, healthcare, and poverty alleviation. Some of its notable achievements include:

SUBTITLE

Philanthropy

Saylani runs extensive food distribution programs, ensuring that countless individuals receive daily meals.

SUBTITLE

Education/IT Courses

The organization has pledged to enroll 10 million out-of-school children in Pakistan and has made significant contributions to IT education, helping Pakistan rank fourth in the World Index of Freelancers

SUBTITLE

Support for Orphans and Widow

It offers shelters and emotional support to vulnerable groups, creating safe havens for personal growth

SUBTITLE

Employement

Saylani organizes job fairs to connect job seekers with employers. For instance, a recent job fair in Karachi resulted in 170 individuals receiving immediate job offers

Touch of Happiness

Welfare has consistently demonstrated a deep commitment to serving the community, providing essential services to those in need.
